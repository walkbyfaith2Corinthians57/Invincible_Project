What's Next & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many seasons will there be?

Creator Robert Kirkman has stated that he envisions the show running for roughly 7 to 8 seasons to fully adapt the entirety of the 144-issue comic book source material properly.

Is this suitable for kids?

Absolutely not. Despite its bright, colorful Saturday morning cartoon aesthetic, the show features extreme, graphic violence, mature themes, and intense emotional trauma. It is rated TV-MA for an incredibly good reason.

Does the show follow the comics exactly?

Mostly, yes. However, Robert Kirkman has used the show as a "second draft," re-ordering certain events for better pacing and expanding the roles of some supporting characters who didn't get enough spotlight in the early comics.

What storylines are coming next?

Without spoiling too much for non-comic readers, upcoming arcs delve deep into the 'Invincible War' and the escalating cosmic conflict with the Viltrumite Empire, introducing terrifying new threats from across the galaxy and forcing Mark to make impossible choices.
