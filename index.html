Think You Know Heroes?

Most heroes save the cat. Mark Grayson has to save the world—from the people he loves most.

Invincible starts as a classic coming-of-age story: a teenager finally getting the powers he always dreamed of. But by the end of the first episode, you’ll realize this isn't a Saturday morning cartoon. It’s a brutal, emotional, and high-stakes epic that asks one terrifying question:

What do you do when the world's greatest protector is its greatest threat?

Across three massive seasons, follow Mark’s journey from a rookie hero to a galactic warrior. No plot armor. No easy wins. Just a father, a son, and a blood-stained path to the truth.

Warning: It gets messy. It gets emotional. And it’s the best thing you’ll watch this year.
