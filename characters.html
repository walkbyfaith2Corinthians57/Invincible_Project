Key Characters

Mark Grayson
Invincible

Abilities: Super strength, flight, immense durability, and a healing factor.

Significance: The heart of the story. A teenager burdened with the legacy of his father's planet. He desperately tries to hold onto his human morality in a universe that constantly tests it.

Nolan Grayson
Omni-Man

Abilities: Essentially the same as Mark, but refined by thousands of years of combat.

Significance: The primary antagonist of the first arc, he is an advance scout for the Viltrumite Empire forced to choose between his mission and his genuine love for his human family.

Samantha Wilkins
Atom Eve

Abilities: Subatomic matter manipulation, energy blasts, flight.

Significance: The emotional anchor and moral compass of the superhero community. She struggles with the limits of her vast powers when confronting unfixable systemic issues.

Cecil Stedman

Abilities: None. Highly intelligent, extremely pragmatic.

Significance: Leader of the GDA, he believes the ends justify the means. His complex relationship with Mark defines the latter's early superhero career.

Allen the Alien

Abilities: Reactive adaptation, super strength, flight, telepathy.

Significance: A key operative for the Coalition of Planets and one of Mark's most loyal and powerful allies. His incredibly jovial nature contrasts his lethal capabilities.

Debbie Grayson

Abilities: Extreme emotional resilience, high intelligence, sharp intuition.

Significance: Mark's human mother and Nolan's wife. She represents the fragility but also the profound strength of humanity, grounding Mark and challenging the Viltrumite ideology.

Oliver Grayson
Kid Omni-Man

Abilities: Super strength, flight, rapidly developing durability.

Significance: Mark's younger half-brother. His rapid aging and different moral upbringing constantly put him at philosophical odds with Mark on how to be a hero.

Battle Beast

Abilities: God-like physical strength, durability, unparalleled combat prowess.

Significance: A warrior from a distant world seeking a worthy opponent. He is one of the few beings in the universe capable of going toe-to-toe with the strongest Viltrumites.

Conquest

Abilities: Overwhelming Viltrumite strength, speed, flight, extreme durability.

Significance: The most brutal and sociopathic enforcer of the Viltrumite Empire. He serves as Mark's ultimate physical and psychological test.

Grand Regent Thragg

Abilities: The absolute pinnacle of Viltrumite power. Unmatched strength, speed, and durability.

Significance: The supreme ruler of the Viltrumite Empire. He is the overarching antagonist of the entire series, ruling through sheer terror and unparalleled might.
