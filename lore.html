The Lore of Invincible

The Viltrumite Empire

Originating from the planet Viltrum, this race of super-powered beings is almost unstoppable. While they claim to be a benevolent empire bringing peace to the galaxy, their true methodology is brutal conquest. The strongest survive, the weak are purged.

The Global Defense Agency (GDA)

A covert government organization led by Cecil Stedman. The GDA monitors superhuman activity on Earth, funds the Guardians of the Globe, and isn't afraid to get its hands permanently dirty to ensure the safety of the planet.

Guardians of the Globe

Earth's premier superhero team, effectively the first line of defense against world-ending threats. Following a devastating massacre, a completely new roster of younger, less experienced heroes must step up.

Coalition of Planets

A rebellion of various alien species uniting against the Viltrumite expansion. Led by Thaedus and employing agents like Allen the Alien, they are desperately seeking any advantage to turn the tide of the war.
