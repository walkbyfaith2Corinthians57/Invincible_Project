Production & Streaming

The Origins

Invincible is based on the groundbreaking comic book series created by writer Robert Kirkman (The Walking Dead) and artists Cory Walker and Ryan Ottley. The comic ran for exactly 144 issues, concluding a massive continuous story arc.

Animation & Style

Produced by Skybound Entertainment and Amazon Studios, the show's art direction intentionally mimics the distinct, clean lines of the original comic while bringing a terrifying weight and kinetic energy to the intense action sequences.

Voice Cast

The series features a wildly talented star-studded ensemble, including Steven Yeun (Mark), J.K. Simmons (Nolan), Sandra Oh (Debbie), Walton Goggins (Cecil), and Gillian Jacobs (Eve), elevating the emotional impact of the script.

Streaming Details

Invincible is available exclusively on Amazon Prime Video. The format utilizes an intense hour-long episodic presentation, allowing scenes to breathe and stakes to properly escalate in a way standard animated shows cannot.
